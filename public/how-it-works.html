The mechanism

Utilities tell you
when they're about to buy.
Most vendors aren't listening.

Every grid budget is preceded by public signals. Mandates land. Grants open. Reliability events shove R&D to the front of the queue. Executives get hired to own a new problem. That's the map of where money is about to move, visible twelve to eighteen months before an RFP lands. The companies that win long-cycle grid deals don't sell harder. They read the public record earlier.

Two tiers of signal

Most vendors run the first row.
We also run the second.

The first row is table stakes. The horizontal intent surface any capable demand team tracks. The second is the grid-specialist layer. The public pressure system that precedes every utility buying decision. Both rows, same engine, your ICP layered on top.

What nobody else is watching. The public pressure system that precedes every grid procurement, visible if you know where to look.

05 High · early

Regulatory & tariff moves

Every mandate creates a deadline. Every deadline creates a ring-fenced budget. Filings are the earliest trace that money is about to move.

  • Grid-connection queue consultations
06 High · specific

Grant & innovation windows

Public funding closes the eighteen-month gap between intent and procurement. The money is already committed. A vendor will be chosen, usually within six months.

Watching
  • Regulator innovation funding rounds
  • Supranational transition grant programs
  • Federal grid infrastructure allocations
07 Very high · ready

Pilots, consortiums & tenders

The buyer has publicly raised their hand. Hardest signals to fake, easiest to act on. Win rates on well-timed responses run an order of magnitude above cold.

Watching
  • Regulator-run innovation sandboxes
  • Major industry research consortium intakes
  • DSO flexibility tender notices & RFI windows
The flywheel

Six stages. One loop.
Every rotation feeds the next.

Signals feed the system. The system feeds the sale. The sale, delivered well, feeds new signals. Clients co-present, get cited in consortiums, show up in the trade press. The wheel spins on work already done.

01
Detect

Signals surface from the public record. Filings, grants, hires, pilots, reliability events. Dozens of sources, continuously.

02
Prioritise

Scored against your ICP. Fit × intent ranks accounts by readiness, not by list size. Rescored nightly against what closed last month.

03
Engage

Outbound references the signal. Conversations open warm because they're timed to the buyer's pressure, not yours.

04
Convert

POC → pilot → commercial, with the procurement motion running in parallel from week one. Pilots that don't die at purchasing.

05
Deliver

Execution so clean the buyer advocates for you internally. This is where most vendors lose the flywheel.

06
Amplify

Case studies, co-presentations and consortium wins become the detection surface for the next round of buyers watching.
